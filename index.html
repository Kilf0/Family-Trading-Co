Introducing BLUEBERRY REPOSE

A Delicious Blend of Apple Cider Vinegar, Local Honey and Real Blueberries!

OUR STORY

We at Family Trading Company are glad to bring to market an Apple Cider Vinegar - Juice drink to be enjoyed by everyone.

In search of a healthy sustainable option to the common nutritional juices, we came across this delicious vinegar juice. It may also be known as Shrub, Drinking Vinegar, Biblical / Ancient Wine (Non-Alcoholic), or Oxymel. While there are many was to make it as there are juice flavors available, we have honed in on a process which preserves as much of the initial ingredient's properties, while making it delicious and healthy

OUR INGREDIENTS

We use only the real products, and nothing from concentrate or unnecessarily processed.

Our initial vinegar juice "Blueberry Repose" is a delicious blend of:

Real Blueberries

Local Honey

Apple Cider Vinegar

OUR PROCESS

Nothing is pasteurized or otherwise processed beyond what we believe the common man would have done in times gone by.

All three ingredients are mixed together at room temperature, allowed to steep and then the pulp is strained out. What is left is the delicious elixir, ready to stimulate your taste buds and promote health in the body.

We recommend our Blueberry Repose Vinegar Juice be mixed with water, at a 1 to 8 ratio, or to taste. (some like it stronger).

The product does not require refrigeration, providing it's kept at room temperature in the sealed container.
